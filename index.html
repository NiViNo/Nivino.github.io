TrendExplorer

Interactive Shiny App – Global News Article Analytics (2010–2025)

TrendExplorer identifies emerging topics and tracks their evolution across clusters of co-occurring keywords. Built on 500+ news articles, it maps semantic networks, detects topic clusters, and forecasts their future relevance. Ideal for strategic foresight, innovation scouting, and agenda setting.
