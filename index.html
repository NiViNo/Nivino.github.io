Berlin, Germany

I work at the intersection of data analysis, applied research, and innovation strategy. I build tools and insights to help organisations make better decisions – from venture data to funding policy.

Selected Projects

Startup Network Explorer

Interactive Shiny App – Crunchbase VC data (global)

Mapped startup ecosystems based on co-investment and industry connections. Network clustering, trend filters, and centrality metrics enable deep ecosystem insights. Used in university research and policy briefings.

VC Trends Dashboard

Dashboard based on public funding and investment data. Used by students and stakeholders in seminars to analyse capital flows and identify key sectors.
